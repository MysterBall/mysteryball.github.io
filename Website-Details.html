How'd it begin?

Mr Mystery, at age five, wondered: 'How do these people make such great names; Gravity Falls, Men in Black, Knight Rider, etc? Let me think... Hm, Galaxy 15! MysteryBall! Yes, I'll write a book with pictures called: Galaxy 15!' So this Mr Mystery hand-wrote a book called Galaxy 15. From here, their imagination expanded, creating a story that went through generations for a lot of years. Then one day Mr M. decided to share this with the world, but the story was too long. Hence, Mr M. re-thought the entire continuity, and put it on this very website for the enjoyment of the sensible world.
